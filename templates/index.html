About Our AI

Our AI agents are built using Deep Q-Network (DQN), a reinforcement learning algorithm that allows them to learn optimal strategies through experience.

The AI agents are trained on both predefined strategies and real player data, allowing them to adapt and improve over time.

By playing against our AI, you're not only challenging yourself but also helping to improve the AI through your gameplay data.
